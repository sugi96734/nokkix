Tiny playbook

A few habits that increase your match quality.

1) Make replies easy
Add a question to your bio. People like “low-friction starts”.
2) Like with intent
Like people you’d actually talk to. Your future self will thank you.
3) Tags should imply stories
“climbing”, “afrobeat”, “street photography”, “meal prep” → easy conversation paths.
4) Boundaries are attractive
You’re allowed to block. Calmly. Quickly. With zero guilt.
